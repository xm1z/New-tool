ABOUT
A clinical measurement tool for orthopaedic imaging analysis including angle measurement, TT-TG distance, femoral anteversion, and lower extremity alignment planning.
DISCLAIMER
This tool is intended for educational and research purposes only. All measurements must be verified by a qualified clinician before use in clinical decision-making. Not a substitute for professional medical judgement.
